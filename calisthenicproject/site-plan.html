Content

Home page

The fact that we have you here means that you have come to learn how to have better management of your body through calisthenics, and it is incredible, we are happy to have you visit us. It is important that you understand what calisthenics is. Calisthenics is a system of exercises where you leave aside the dumbbells, discs, or bars (for lifting). You will focus on using your own body weight to complete the exercises. With this, you can obtain some benefits that are very important such as gaining flexibility and mobility, improving coordination, increasing muscular resistance, reducing pain due to poor posture, increasing functional strength, etc. What do you need to start practicing calisthenics? Remember that your main team will be your body itself, as it is with the aim of obtaining greater control over your body. However, there are other things you can add to your workouts, such as training with rings, pull-up bars, etc. Once you start learning the basics, nothing will stop you. The best thing about calisthenics is that you don't need a specific place to practice it, you can do it anywhere, even if at first it seems too difficult or even impossible, if you use your diligence and patience you will see how little by little you learn to master even the most complicated exercises in the world of calisthenics.

Training

This is the place where you can learn how to improve your techniques when performing some of the most famous and well-known exercises in the world of calisthenics, it is essential to remember that the only thing that will really help you to perform them to perfection is perseverance, so now that you have started this path do not stop. Starting with the basic exercises, are those that can help anyone who wants to enter the world of calisthenics, these exercises help to strengthen certain parts of the body that later will help you to do exercises with greater difficulty, this is a phase that you can enjoy a lot because you are discovering the diversity of exercises that exist and you are creating your own routine, it is important that you focus on those parts in which you feel that it is difficult for you to work more. Within this category, we can include what are pull-ups, push-ups, squads, etc. Static exercises, are exercises with a higher difficulty, having a focus on keeping the body in a certain position for a period of time. Coordination and technique are essential when mastering this type of exercise. Among them are the Full planche, the flag, the front lever, the back lever, L sit, etc.

Equipment

Here we will also show you some of the equipment you can use to have a better performance when performing these exercises. The equipment is essential to facilitating the activity, but it is not a must-have. Remember, he who wants to can! A very important part of exercising with calisthenics is learning to master the training bars, that is, being able to perform a variety of exercises with them. You may be wondering why? Well, thanks to the training bars you will be able to train your chest, back, abdomen, legs, and arms. Sets for calisthenics training, are very useful because they offer several options so that you can work different parts of your body such as the abdomen, leg, forearm, etc. There are some training kits that include the following: jump rope, push-up rack, abdominal wheel, etc. However remember that the kits are not limited to only those training equipment, some may include other things not mentioned here. So now you know, the world of calisthenics is very open and there are many things that you can enjoy.
